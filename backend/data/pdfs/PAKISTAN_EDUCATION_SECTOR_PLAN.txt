PAKISTAN EDUCATION SECTOR PLAN 2023-2027

Vision Statement
Transform Pakistan's education system to produce globally competitive, socially responsible citizens 
equipped with 21st-century skills.

Current Status
- Primary enrollment: 67%
- Teacher vacancy: 312,000 positions
- School infrastructure gap: 43,000 schools needed
- Gender parity index: 0.62

Strategic Objectives
1. Increase primary enrollment from 67% to 90% by 2027
2. Recruit and train 150,000 teachers
3. Build/upgrade 50,000 schools
4. Introduce STEM education in 5,000 schools

Policy Focus Areas
- Rural education development
- Girls' education and safety
- Technical vocational training
- Special education inclusion
- Digital literacy programs
