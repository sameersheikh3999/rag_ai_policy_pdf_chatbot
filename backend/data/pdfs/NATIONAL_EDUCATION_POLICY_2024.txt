NATIONAL EDUCATION POLICY 2024

Executive Summary
This policy framework aims to transform education delivery across all levels through innovation, 
equity, and quality. Key objectives include universal primary enrollment, digital transformation, 
and teacher capacity development.

Strategic Pillars
1. Equitable Access: Ensure 95% enrollment in primary education
2. Quality Learning: Improve learning outcomes through evidence-based practices  
3. Digital Integration: Deploy technology in all schools by 2027
4. Teacher Excellence: Certify and train 50,000 educators annually

Financial Framework
Government allocation: 6% of national budget
Private partnerships: 15% additional funding
International support: 5% through grants and loans

Implementation Timeline
Phase 1 (2024-2025): Foundation and infrastructure
Phase 2 (2025-2026): Scaling teacher training programs  
Phase 3 (2026-2027): Technology deployment
Phase 4 (2027-2028): Monitoring and evaluation

Expected Outcomes
- 90% primary completion rate
- 85% literacy rate in underserved areas
- 40% improvement in learning assessments
- Gender parity in all education levels
